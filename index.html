Group Presentation Activity

Understanding the American Education System

Instructions

  • Divide the class into 2 groups of 5 students each.
  • Each group prepares a 7–10 minute presentation.
  • Use slides, visuals, or posters to support your explanation.
  • Each member should speak during the presentation.
  • Include 1 discussion question to ask your classmates.
Group 1: Structure and School Life
  • Levels of Education: Pre-K, K–12, Postsecondary
  • School Types: Public, Private, Special Needs
  • Typical School Day: Hours, subjects, organization
  • Graduation Requirements: Credits, GPA, diploma
  • Parental Involvement & Student Values
Group 2: Access, Testing & Pathways
  • Funding & Government Role
  • Standardized Tests: SAT, ACT
  • College Admission Requirements
  • Gifted and Special Programs
  • Responsibility, Behavior, and School Culture

Evaluation Criteria
